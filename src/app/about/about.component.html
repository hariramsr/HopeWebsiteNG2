About Us

About

Helping Out Public Effectively (HOPE) is an organisation founded by youth in August 2015.

Our Objectives

  • To provide educational support to the poor students
  • To render assistance to the orphans for their livelihood
  • To provide medical support to the poor needy people
  • To educate and motive people to keep their neighbourhood clean and to contribute for Clean India.

Education

"HOPE believes that Education is the effective weapon to fight poverty and truly empower the children". HOPE provides the poor students their basic needs like Bags, Books, Pens, Pencils, Instrument boxes and other basic infrastructure to carry forward their education effectively. Exposing the rural students to the technology, ongoing events around world and helping them to catch to the speed of urban students by its TEACHING initiative..

Health

"HOPE believes that there is direct link between poverty and ill health." HOPE took initiative in this matter and provides the health services to the people staying in Rural Areas by organizing Health Camps. The uniqueness of our health camps is that it will be arranged in villages where health services are not available easily. We conduct free checkups for the needy people and prescribe the proper treatment and provide the free medicines to them.

Swachh Bharath

“HOPE believes that Cleanliness is next to godliness.” HOPE took initiative in contributing to the PM Modi’s Swachh Bharath Mission Program by installing the dustbins across the Kolar Town.

Save the Environment

"HOPE believes that a healthy environment creates a healthy life." HOPE took initiative in this regard by doing plantations in schools, orphanages we visit. HOPE organized the installation of feeders (food and water) for birds across the Kolar Town.

Rural Transformation

"HOPE believes that the future of India lies in villages." India will be developed only if its rural areas are developed. HOPE focuses on enriching the lives of rural people by providing them the basic facilities such as education, health etc.

Nurture the Old

“HOPE believes in keeping our elders happy, so that their blessings will be with us till our last breath." HOPE focuses on to improve the quality of life of destitute elders primarily staying in various old age homes by providing them rations, Medicines and consultations to them.
